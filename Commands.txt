🔧 Quick PM2 health checks / restart commands
Excellent progress — your setup is on the right track and just needs polish.
Use these commands at any time in your project folder:

1) Is it running?
pm2 status
pm2 list
2) View logs
pm2 logs reseller-app --lines 50
pm2 logs reseller-app --err (error-only)
pm2 logs reseller-app --out (stdout-only)
3) Restart app
pm2 restart reseller-app
(or rollback with fresh start:)
pm2 stop reseller-app
pm2 delete reseller-app
pm2 start ecosystem.config.js
4) Stop cleanly
pm2 stop reseller-app
pm2 delete reseller-app
pm2 flush (clear old logs)
5) Check current command plus environment
pm2 show reseller-app
